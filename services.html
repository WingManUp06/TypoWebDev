Our Craft and Expertice

Stratigize

At Typo Web Dev we always follow checklists to ensure everything is done. We strategize everything to ensure customer satisfaction. When developing applications, we follow the Software Development Life Cycle. This cycle consists of planning, defining, designing, building, testing and deployment. We ensure that we don't skip any steps and that you the customer are receiving a quality product

Design

At Typo Web Dev, we believe that design plays a crucial role in the success of a website. That's why we place a high emphasis on creating visually appealing and user-friendly designs that are sure to capture the attention of visitors and keep them engaged. We understand that the longer a user stays on a website, the more likely they are to convert, and that's why we focus on creating designs that not only look good but are also optimized for conversions.

Code

We guarantee that all our code is clean and easy to work with if maintenance is ever necessary. We run our code through validators which ensure that the code will work without error. This minimizes the downtime your website could have. When you receive one of our websites you are receiving a quality product.
